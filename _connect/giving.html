Why give?

For us, giving is about much more than simply paying bills. There is something much deeper to the "why" we give. Here some of the primary reasons we believe financial generosity matters:

  1. Being generous and joyfully sharing the resources God has provided for us is a transformative practice and a significant act of worship as it displays the heart of the God we serve.
  2. Generosity is a tangible measure of our journey into trusting Jesus fully as we learn to live within our means and with open hands and hearts in regards to our resources.
  3. Your generosity changes the lives of people in Edwardsville, the surrounding communities, and in various places around the globe as we work together for the redeeming, reconciling and restoring of the world around us.
