Our Services

One of the greatest compliments we receive is when someone tells us that from the very first time they walked through our doors they felt welcome and at ease in our "living room". Our greatest hope is that each aspect of the service helps create an environment that allows people to come as they are and safely explore a faith in Jesus regardless of where they are in that journey.

We invite you to join us in our "living room" any given Sunday at 9:15 or 11:00 a.m. and hope that you too will feel like you "belong".
